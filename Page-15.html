ABOUT

Areekode is a town on the banks of the Chaliyar River in Areekode Grama Panchayat in Malappuram district India. Areekode was part of the Malabar district in British India.It is a town on the banks of Chaliyar River with an area of 7.25 square miles, Areekode is one of the highly literate areas in the state of Kerala. Situated in the Northern part of Malappuram district, it is encircled by hills and hillocks capped in green. The Areekode region includes Urangatiri, Kizhuparamba, Kavanoor and Areekode panchayats

KollamKolli

Kollam Kolli Falls is a beautiful waterfall located near Areekode in Malappuram district. It is a beautiful place that is not visited by many tourists. Water is available only during the monsoons. The view from the top of the hill in three layers is very beautiful.

kakkadampoyil

Kakkadampoyil is situated in koodaranhi Panchayat in kozhikode District near Areekode. This hilltop village is one of the coolest tourist destinations in Malabar region

Puthalam

Puthalam is a small Village/hamlet in Areakode Block in Malappuram District of Kerala State, India. It comes under Areacode Panchayath. It belongs to North Kerala Division
